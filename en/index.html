The Meaning of Easter

Why This Night Matters

✝️

The Victory of Life

Easter is not simply an ancient tradition. It is the proclamation that on this night, death was defeated — once and for all. The tomb was empty. Christ is alive. And this changes everything.

🕯️

An Open Invitation

Whether you are Orthodox, Christian of another tradition, or simply curious — you are welcome. Come with a candle. Come with your questions. Come with your heart.

⛪

Orthodox Church

Our parish follows the Eastern Orthodox tradition. The Paschal service is sung in Church Slavonic and includes the Midnight Office, the Procession, Matins, and Divine Liturgy.
